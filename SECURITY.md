## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability within Otto, please follow these steps:

1.  **Do not open a public issue.**
2.  Email the maintainers directly.
3.  Include a detailed description of the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeline for a fix. We appreciate your responsible disclosure and will credit you for your discovery once the vulnerability has been patched.
